Community Guidelines

Help us keep AlMaGen-Arena welcoming, safe, and positive for all players.

Respect Everyone

  • No abusive, hateful, or harassing behavior.
  • Use clean and friendly language in feedback/comments.

Fair Play

  • No cheating tools, exploit sharing, or score manipulation attempts.
  • Report bugs responsibly through the support channel.

Safety First

  • Do not share private personal information in public areas.
  • Parental guidance is recommended for younger players.

Content Standards

  • No spam, fake reports, or promotional abuse.
  • Respect copyrights and platform policies.
