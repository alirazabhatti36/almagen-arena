🔒 Privacy Policy

📅 Last Updated: July 8, 2026

⚡ Quick Summary: We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is 100% safe with us. No sign-ups, no tracking, no data collection.

1. Information We Collect

None. AlMaGen-Arena does not collect any personal information. We do not require sign-ups, logins, or any form of user registration.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to use. Your gaming experience is completely private and anonymous.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies only for:

  • Game Progress: To save your scores locally in your browser (localStorage). This data never leaves your device.
  • Ad Preferences: Google AdSense may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can manage this through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: For displaying relevant advertisements. They may use cookies as per their Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics: For anonymous traffic analysis. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

5. Data Storage

All game data (scores, progress, achievements) is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We have no servers and no databases — your data never leaves your device.

6. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

  • ✅ Right to Access: Check your browser's localStorage
  • ✅ Right to Delete: Clear your browser cache
  • ✅ Right to Opt-out: Use "Do Not Consent" option in our CMP

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

AlMaGen-Arena is a family-friendly platform. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. All games are educational and safe for all ages.

8. No Gambling, No Betting

🚫 100% Gambling-Free: AlMaGen-Arena does not support or promote any form of gambling, betting, or real-money gaming. All games are purely for entertainment and educational purposes.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

📧 info@almagen-arena.com

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
