DMCA Policy

This page explains how AlMaGen-Arena handles copyright complaints under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

Filing a Copyright Complaint

If you believe copyrighted material is available on this site without authorization, send a notice with:

  • Your full name and contact details.
  • Identification of the copyrighted work.
  • The exact URL of the allegedly infringing content.
  • A statement that you have a good-faith belief the use is unauthorized.
  • A statement that your notice is accurate under penalty of perjury.

Submission Contact

Email: info@almagen-arena.com

Response Process

Valid requests are reviewed and processed promptly. We may remove or disable the reported content while evaluation is in progress.

Counter Notice

If content was removed by mistake, the uploader may submit a counter notice with required legal details.
