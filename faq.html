Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about gameplay, progress, rewards, and platform usage.

Do I need to register to play games?

No. All games are playable without registration in Phase 1.

How does Continue Playing work?

It is a static preview section right now. Backend-linked continuation is planned for later phases.

Are favorites synced across devices?

Favorites are currently a UI/local-storage placeholder. Account sync will be added in a backend phase.

Can I report a broken game?

Yes. Every game page includes a report button to contact support quickly.

Is AlMaGen-Arena free?

Yes, the current catalog is free to play with no required downloads.
